◆ CameraTrackball()

inviwo::CameraTrackball::CameraTrackball ( CameraProperty * cameraProp)

Convenience class for trackball using camera properties. Calls propertyModified() on camera when any of the positions or vectors change. Rotates and moves the camera around a sphere. This object does not take ownership of pointers handed to it.

See also
Trackball
Parameters
cameraPropPointer to the camera that will be controlled.
